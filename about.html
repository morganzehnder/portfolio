WHY UX?

WHY UX?

With a background in economics and account management, my path to design was not a direct one. A lifelong passion for interior design and architecture, combined with later formed interests in art and graphic design, led me to seek my own form of creative expression.

As a sales and marketing professional in Sports-Tech, I assisted clients using an SaaS recruitment platform. Seeing clients struggle firsthand and representing their interests as Product Bridge, I discovered the importance of user-centric product design. During my 6 month UX Design Apprenticeship, I refined my user research skills and developed a passion for creating colorful, engaging user experiences.

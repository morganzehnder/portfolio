Why UX?

Why UX?

With a background in economics and account management, my path to design was not a direct one. A lifelong passion for interior design and architecture, combined with later formed interests in art and graphic design, led me to seek my own form of creative expression.

As an account manager in the tech industry, I spent my time assisting clients using an SaaS recruitment platform. Seeing their struggles firsthand and working with the product team to make improvements, I peeked into the dynamic world of UX Design and decided to align my creative interests and my career.
